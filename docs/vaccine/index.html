Vaccination data reported daily by the Texas Department of Health State Services are used for all metrics. Raw historical data are aggregated here. The number of vaccine doses administered, number of people vaccinated with one dose, and number of people fully vaccinated are aggregated by the recipient's county of residence. The number of doses shipped and number of doses allocated are aggregated by provider county. The number of doses allocated is updated weekly and includes doses that will be delivered during the upcoming week. Health care providers have 24 hours after a dose is administered to enter information into the Texas Immunization Registry (ImmTrac2). The daily data comes from vaccination records submitted by health care providers as of 11:59 PM the previous night. Population numbers are based on 2019 estimates from the U.S. Census Bureau.
